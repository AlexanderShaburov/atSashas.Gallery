About the Exhibition

Twelve works exploring the line between where we've been and what we remember of it.

The paintings in this collection began as studies of real places — coastlines, markets, train stations — but over months of reworking, they moved away from representation toward something more felt than seen. What remains is colour, gesture, and the residue of attention.

The exhibition brings together work from the past two years, much of it shown for the first time.
