Plein Air Session

Painting the Ligurian Coast

Two days of open-air watercolor on the Italian Riviera

The fishing villages between Camogli and Portofino offer something rare — light that changes every ten minutes.

Section 2 — Description required

Over two mornings, we'll set up along the harbour and coastal path, working in watercolor and gouache. Afternoons are free — to review the work, explore, or simply sit and sketch.

This is not a course with assignments. It's a shared experience of painting in a place that rewards attention. All levels welcome.
