What You'll Explore

This workshop is built around the fundamentals that make watercolor both challenging and rewarding: wet-on-wet technique, color mixing directly on paper, and learning to work with — rather than against — the medium's unpredictability.

You'll leave with two to three finished studies, a personal color mixing chart, and the confidence to continue experimenting at home. All materials are provided, including professional-grade paints and paper.

Designed for beginners and those returning to watercolor after a break. Maximum 8 participants for individual attention.
